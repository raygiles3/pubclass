About Me

Ray, The Web Instructor

Ray is a passionate and dedicated web development instructor with over a decade of experience in the tech industry. He specializes in making complex topics like modern JavaScript, responsive CSS, and backend architecture accessible to learners of all levels. Ray believes in a hands-on, project-based approach to education, empowering his students to build impressive, real-world applications. When he's not coding or teaching, Ray is an active community advocate, dedicating his time to local improvement projects.
